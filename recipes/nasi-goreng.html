Nasi Goreng Recipe


Nasi goreng, or Indonesian fried rice, is a beloved dish made from stir-fried rice, typically with a savory-sweet soy sauce base. It often includes scrambled eggs, vegetables like carrots and peas, and a protein such as chicken, shrimp, or tofu. Served hot, it's a flavorful and satisfying meal.


Ingredients:

  • Rice: Preferably cold or leftover rice from the previous day.
  • Ground Spices: Garlic, shallots, chili, and shrimp paste (optional).
  • Sweet Soy Sauce: For sweetness and characteristic color.
  • Cooking Oil: For sautéing the spices.
  • Protein: Chicken, shrimp, or egg (choose one or mix).
  • Garnishes: Sliced cucumber and tomato.
  • Crackers: For a crispy texture on top of the fried rice.


Steps:

  1. Sauté the minced garlic, shallots, and chilies until fragrant.
  2. Add your chosen protein (chicken, shrimp, or egg) and cook until done.
  3. Stir in the cooked rice, breaking up any clumps.
  4. Add sweet soy sauce and mix well until the rice is evenly coated.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with sliced cucumber and tomato.
  7. Enjoy with a side of crackers (kerupuk).
