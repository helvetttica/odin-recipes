Rujak Recipe


Rujak is a vibrant Indonesian fruit salad, typically featuring a medley of fresh, unripe fruits like mango, papaya, and pineapple. It's tossed in a sweet, spicy, and tangy peanut sauce, often with a hint of palm sugar and chili, offering a refreshing and addictive flavor profile.


Ingredients:

  • Young Mango
  • Water Apple (Jambu Air)
  • Cucumber
  • Kedondong
  • Pineapple
  • Jicama (Bengkuang)

Peanut Sauce Ingredients:
  • Fried Peanuts
  • Palm Sugar
  • Bird's Eye Chili
  • Shrimp Paste (Terasi, optional)
  • Tamarind Water or Plain Water
  • Salt


Steps:

  1. Prepare all the fresh fruits. Wash them thoroughly.
  2. Peel and cut the fruits into bite-sized pieces.
  3. For the sauce, grind chili, garlic, and shrimp paste (if using) into a paste.
  4. In a mortar or bowl, mix the ground spices with palm sugar, salt, and tamarind water until well combined.
  5. Taste the sauce and adjust the sweetness, spiciness, and sourness to your preference.
  6. Arrange the cut fruits on a plate.
  7. Pour the peanut sauce over the fruits just before serving.
